TEDx Volunteer

Hello everyone, I am excited to share that I had an opportunity to participate as a logistics volunteer at a TEDx event. Being part of such a prestigious platform allowed me to engage with inspiring speakers mainly Ivan Rodrigues & Nidhi Nahata, gaining valuable insights from their diverse perspectives and innovative ideas. This experience not only enhanced my organizational and communication skills but also broadened my understanding of various global issues. I am grateful for the privilege of contributing to an event that fosters knowledge sharing and community engagement, and I look forward to applying these learnings in my future endeavors. Extending my thanks to TEDxMSRIT and anticipating many more opportunities ahead.
